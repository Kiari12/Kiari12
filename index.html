Kendo Classes


We offer kendo classes for all skill levels. If you're new to kendo, you'll start in the beginner class. You can move up as you show progress in your skills. For everyone, we focus on mastering the basics and building a strong foundation. This way, you can improve effectively and enjoy your kendo journey!

Beginner


Learn the basic etiquette, manners, and foundations of kendo.

Intermediate


Build upon what you have learned from the beginning quarter, combining techniques and applying them further.

Advance


Utilize advanced techniques in real scenarios such as sparring, tournaments, and grade testings.
